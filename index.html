// 01 - About

WHO I AM

Hello - I'm Benjamin. Im a Video Game developer and Concept Artist with a love for visual creativity. Thinking outside of the box whatever i build weather it be artwork for a book series or an action packed video I will make you see thing you wont soon forget.

Wether you are looking for something as specific as a fantasy epic book, with fantasy ethnicities in a Cyberpunk setting, to time traveling redneck engineers fighting off a robot apocolypse using osha violations as weapons. Video Games that you never could have imagined, to art that you never seen before, I will build things in an unconventinal way that will make you see things from a new perspective.

I work in digital painting, manga/ink, pixel art, and traditional media, and I bring those visuals into interactive experiences through game development along with strong directing skills.
